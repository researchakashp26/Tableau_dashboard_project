# Tableau_dashboard_project
Mumbai region-wise Air Quality Indexes Dashboard (2017-23) - (Tableau Public) 

Extracted and analysed AQI dataset covering PM2.5 and PM10 pollution levels across 3 major Mumbai wards - Central Mumbai (Sion), Western Mumbai (Bandra) and South Mumbai (Colaba) - covering 7 years from 2017 to 2023 to identify regional air quality patterns.

• Created Calculated Fields to compute yearly averages and categorise AQI readings into Poor, Satisfactory and Good classifications - built interactive charts, pie charts, slicers and filters enabling dynamic drill-down analysis across wards and years.

• Identified Central Mumbai (Sion) as the most critically polluted ward with consistently Poor AQI ratings across 2017–2023, and established that PM10 contributed the largest average percentage of pollutants compared to PM2.5 - insights visualised through an interactive Tableau dashboard with KPIs for easy interpretation.
